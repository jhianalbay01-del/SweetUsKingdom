Kiamoy

Kiamoy is a popular Filipino snack made from dried, preserved fruits like plums, apricots, or prunes that are treated with brine and vinegar, resulting in a sweet, sour, and salty flavor. It is also known as li hing mui or huamei in some regions and is often sold with a coating of powder made from ingredients like anise, salt, and sugar. The snack is sold as whole dried fruit, strips, or as a powder for flavoring other snacks and fruits.
